Eggs Over Medium

Description

Eggs cooked to perfection (i.e., runny center)!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. Butter pan and set over medium heat.
  2. Crack eggs into pan.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper, to taste.
  4. Cook for 3-1/2 minutes.
  5. Flip, and cook for 2 minutes.
