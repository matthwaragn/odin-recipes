Hot Chocolate French Toast

Description

French toast cooked in a hot chocolate custard!

Ingredients

  • 4 Slices Brioche bread
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 Tablespoon Hot Chocolate
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • Maple Syrup (Optional)

Steps

  1. Mix egg and hot chocolate into a bowl.
  2. Mix in vanilla and cinnamon.
  3. Melt butter in pan over medium heat.
  4. Dip bread into the custard.
  5. Place bread into pan and cook for 1-1/2 minutes, on each side.
